Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free roof estimates?

Yes. Every estimate from LEM Roofing Co. is completely free with no obligation. We'll inspect your roof and provide a clear, written quote.

Do you work with homeowner's insurance claims?

Yes. We have experience working directly with insurance adjusters on storm and hail damage claims, and can help document damage for your claim.

How long does a typical roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements are completed in one to two days, weather permitting. Larger or more complex roofs may take longer — we'll give you a clear timeline up front.

What areas do you service?

We proudly serve Houston and surrounding communities including Katy, Sugar Land, Pearland, The Woodlands, Cypress, Spring, Pasadena, and Baytown.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes. We stand behind our craftsmanship with a workmanship warranty, in addition to manufacturer warranties on materials. Ask your estimator for full details.
